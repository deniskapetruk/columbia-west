Apply As An Initial Overseas Student
‍(applying from your home country)

  • Copy of passport
  • Copy of high school or university diploma or transcript

  • Financial statements showing:

    • $24,500 for 1-year Form I-20*
    • $13,600 for 6-month Form I-20*
    • $6,700 for 3-month Form I-20*
  • Affidavit of Support form, if you're applying with a sponsor (optional)

The financial statements must be dated within the last 3 months.
*For dependents, must show an additional $5,000.00 per dependent.

Apply As A Transfer Student
(currently a student at another school)

  • Copy of passport, F-1 Student Visa and Form I-94
  • Current Form I-20
  • Copy of high school or university diploma or transcript

  • Financial statements showing:

    • $24,500 for 1-year Form I-20*
    • $13,600 for 6-month Form I-20*
    • $6,700 for 3-month Form I-20*
  • Affidavit of Support form, if you're applying with a sponsor (optional)
  • Completed transfer request form (CWC will provide)

The financial statements must be dated within the last 3 months.
*For dependents, must show an additional $5,000.00 per dependent.

Change My Visa Status
(in the US with a non-student visa)

  • Copy of passport and Form I-94
  • Copy of high school or university diploma or transcript

  • Financial statements showing:

    • $24,500 for 1-year Form I-20*
    • $13,600 for 6-month Form I-20*
  • Affidavit of Support form, if you're applying with a sponsor (optional)

The financial statements must be dated within the last 3 months.
*For dependents, must show an additional $5,000.00 per dependent.
