What Makes CWC Teachers Different

Excellence + Empathy

At CWC, we understand that the quality of your teacher has a huge impact on your classroom experience and how quickly you can improve your English. For that reason, CWC takes great pride and care in hiring the best teachers and providing ongoing training through regular classroom observations and monthly professional development workshops.

In addition, CWC's expert and empathetic teachers provide you with the following benefits:

  1. Experience
    • CWC teachers not only have a deep understanding of English concepts, they can explain difficult concepts accurately and in a way that you can understand.
  2. Effective Learning
    • CWC teachers use creative and engaging teaching methods and technology to motivate you and make learning English easier and more fun!
  3. Empathy
    • CWC teachers create a safe and supportive learning environment and build relationships of trust and respect, so your motivation and confidence increase dramatically.
  4. Excellence Sets High Standards
    • CWC teachers set high expectations and challenge you to work hard and try your best!
